Players must analyze the layout of the battlefield, including obstacles, hazards, and advantageous positions, to position their characters strategically and gain the upper hand in combat.

  • Ability Management: Characters in Honkai Star Rail possess various abilities that can turn the tide of battle. Players must manage their characters' abilities wisely, considering factors such as cooldown times, resource costs, and synergy between skills. Timing the activation of abilities can maximize their impact on the outcome of battles.

  • Target Priority: Prioritizing targets is a key tactical decision in battles. Players must assess the threat level of enemy units and prioritize eliminating high-priority targets or disrupting enemy strategies. Choosing the right targets to focus on can swing the momentum of battles in favor of the player's team.

Players must adapt their strategies on the fly, reacting to changes in the battlefield, enemy movements, and unexpected events. Being able to adjust tactics and capitalize on opportunities can turn the tide of seemingly unwinnable battles.
