Honkai : Star Rail

Honkai: Star Rail (Chinese: 崩坏：星穹铁道; pinyin: Bēnghuài: Xīngqióng Tiědào; lit. 'Honkai: Star Dome Railway') is a role-playing gacha video game developed by miHoYo, published by miHoYo in mainland China and worldwide by Cognosphere, d/b/a HoYoverse.

miHoYo's first turn-based game, features the main character, referred to as the "Trailblazer", traveling across worlds through the Astral Express to resolving disasters caused by the "Stellaron". The first closed beta test was launched on October 27, 2021. It was publicly released internationally on April 26, 2023

Honkai Star Rail combines immersive storytelling with strategic gameplay elements, offering players a unique blend of turn-based tactics and card-based combat. Assemble a formidable team of heroes, each possessing their own distinct abilities and personalities, and lead them into battle against a myriad of otherworldly threats.



Engage in tactical showdowns where every decision matters, from choosing the right cards to deploying your units strategically on the battlefield. With each encounter, players must adapt to new challenges, harnessing the power of their heroes and mastering the intricacies of the game's mechanics to emerge victorious.



But Honkai Star Rail is more than just a game—it's an experience. Immerse yourself in the breathtaking visuals and captivating soundscapes that bring the universe to life, and lose yourself in a world where adventure awaits at every turn. Whether you're a seasoned strategist or a newcomer to the genre, Honkai Star Rail offers an unforgettable gaming experience that will keep you coming back for more. So grab your cards, rally your heroes, and prepare to embark on the journey of a lifetime in Honkai Star Rail.
